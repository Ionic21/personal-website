My Favorite Places in Hyderabad

Charminar

Charminar

Built in 1591, this iconic monument is the symbol of Hyderabad. The name comes from its four (char) minarets. The surrounding bazaar is famous for pearl shops and traditional Hyderabadi bangles.

Durgam Cheruvu Cable Bridge

Durgam Cheruvu Cable Bridge

This bridge is a stunning architectural marvel in Hyderabad, gracefully spanning the serene waters like a ribbon of light, seamlessly connecting the city's bustling tech hubs with modern elegance.
